## 0. Brand voice guide

**Positioning in one line (FR):** *Le lagon caché au cœur de la forêt — une journée balnéaire pensée, à 10 minutes d'Alger.*

### Five principles
1. **Honest premium, not hype.** We earn aspiration with specificity, not superlatives. We never promise what operations can't deliver (spotless luxury); we promise the *experience* we genuinely give (lagoon, beds, music, sunset, service-at-the-bed).
2. **Transparent by design.** Price is stated plainly and proudly. "Une grille claire, aucun frais caché" is a brand value, not a footnote — it directly answers our biggest public objection.
3. **Sensory and concrete.** Nouns the visitor can feel — *sable fin, eau turquoise, transats à l'ombre des pins, coucher de soleil sur le lagon* — beat adjectives like *incroyable / exclusif*.
4. **Warm, never exclusionary.** Premium but inclusive. We welcome families, couples, friends and companies. Arrogant "VIP-only" tone backfires given price sensitivity.
5. **We listen, and it shows.** Reviews are how we improve, out in the open. The brand has a humble, active relationship with feedback — turning our weakness into trust.

### Voice: do / don't
| Do (use) | Don't (avoid) |
|---|---|
| lagon, forêt, évasion, à 10 minutes | paradis, the best, n°1 mondial |
| pensé, sur mesure, soigné, espacé | impeccable, immaculé, parfait, spotless |
| une grille claire, aucun frais caché, devis indicatif | dès seulement, à partir de (price-baiting), petits prix |
| réservez à l'avance, votre pass, à votre bed | exclusif réservé à l'élite, VIP ultra-luxe |
| ambiance, coucher de soleil, service au transat | folie, incroyable, magique (empty hype) |
| vos avis font tourner l'île, on s'améliore | (never claim flawless cleanliness/service) |
| et (always spelled out) | & |
| DA (display), DZD (technical) | € or $ for local prices |

**Register:** Fraunces (display) carries warmth and character; keep headlines short, evocative, slightly literary. Manrope (body) carries clarity; keep body copy plain, reassuring, scannable. Eyebrows stay short, uppercase, tracked.
